New York-Style Cheesecake

Description

New York-style cheesecake is larger and richer than traditional cheesecake, thanks to the additions of cream cheese and more egg yolks. Some recipes, like this one, feature sour cream to make the dessert even more decadent.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to make this New York-style cheesecake recipe.

  • For the crust:
    • graham cracker crumbs
    • melted butter
  • For the filling:
    • sour cream
    • all-purpose flour
    • vanilla extract
    • cream cheese
    • white sugar
    • milk
    • eggs
    • lemon zest
    • orange zest

Steps

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade New York-style cheesecake:

  1. Make the crust and press it into a prepared springform pan.
  2. Make the filling and pour it over the crust.
  3. Bake until the edges are puffed and the center is slightly jiggly.
  4. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven.
